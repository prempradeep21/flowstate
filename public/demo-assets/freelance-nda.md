# Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement

**Openhouse Learning Pvt. Ltd.** (the Client), Bengaluru, India
**Prem Pradeep** (the Consultant), Bangkok, Thailand

Effective date: **April 28, 2026**

---

## 1. Purpose

The parties wish to explore a design engagement covering the redesign of the
Client's parent-facing mobile application (the Project) and, in connection
with the Project, may disclose confidential information to each other.

## 2. Confidential Information

Confidential Information means product roadmaps, unreleased designs, user
research, pricing, financial information, and any material marked confidential
at the time of disclosure.

## 3. Obligations

Each party shall (a) use Confidential Information solely for the Project,
(b) protect it with at least the same care as its own confidential material,
and (c) not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

## 4. Term

This Agreement remains in effect for **two (2) years** from the effective
date. Obligations survive completion or termination of the Project.

## 5. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. Disputes shall be subject to
the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Bengaluru.
