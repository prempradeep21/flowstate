# Openhouse — Parent App Redesign · Brief

**Client:** Openhouse Learning, Bengaluru
**Designer:** Prem Pradeep (Bangkok, remote)
**Window:** May 4 – June 22, 2026
**Budget:** ₹4,80,000 (milestone-billed, INR)

---

## The ask

Redesign the parent-facing Openhouse app around discovery and booking of
after-school classes. Parents should get from *what does my child need*
to a booked trial class in under three minutes.

## Scope

- Parent onboarding flow (sign-up → child profile → first booking)
- Class discovery: browse, search, and teacher profiles
- Booking + payment flow, INR pricing throughout
- Design system refresh: tokens, type scale, component library

## Out of scope

- Teacher-side app, admin console, native tablet layouts

## Milestones

1. **Kick off** — May 4 · goals, stakeholders, access
2. **Discovery** — week of May 11 · parent interviews, UX audit
3. **Scope alignment** — May 18 · locked flows and success metrics
4. **Design** — May 25 → Jun 5 · wireframes to hi-fi
5. **Review** — week of Jun 8 · parent testing, iteration
6. **Delivery** — Jun 22 · handoff, specs, tokens

## Success metrics

- Trial-class conversion **+20%** on the new onboarding
- Time-to-first-booking under **3 minutes** (median)
- App Store rating recovers to **4.5+** by end of Q3

## References

- Current app store listing and parent reviews
- Nothing OS 3.0 / 4.0 — tone for motion and type
- Android UI kit (Figma) as the component baseline
