# Guinness 0.0 — tone of voice (working)

> **Fictional spec pitch.** This is *The Liberties*' own working interpretation for a
> demo campaign, not Guinness's real brand guidelines and not a real agency document.
> The Liberties does not exist.

Use this skill for anything written in the brand's voice on this canvas — lines,
supers, scripts, social copy, deck headings, client replies.

## The proposition

**Guinness 0.0 is not a compromise. It is the same 119.5 seconds.**

Everything written here has to be true of the product before it is true of the drinker.

## How it sounds

- **Short.** If a line survives being cut in half, cut it in half.
- **Flat, not clever.** The drama is in the thing, not the sentence. No puns on "zero".
- **Patient.** The brand has waited forty years to say "good things come to those who
  wait" and meant it. Do not sound in a hurry.
- **Dry.** Irish dry, not American warm. Understatement carries it.
- **Never apologetic.** 0.0 is not a lesser pint. Nothing is "still" or "just" or "even".

## Words we use

pour · wait · settle · surge · patience · ruby · the same · hold

## Words we do not

guilt-free · alternative · substitute · mocktail · sober-curious · "why not?" ·
"all the taste, none of the..." · anything that begins "for those moments when"

## Rules

1. **Never sell absence.** The category sells what has been removed. We sell what
   is still there. If a line only works because of what is missing, it is the
   wrong line.
2. **Never explain the drinker.** No reasons, no excuses, no designated-driver
   punchlines. The person holding it owes nobody a sentence.
3. **The wait is real — do not oversell it.** The pour takes as long as it takes.
   Say so once. Do not say it twice.
4. **It is not black.** Held to the light it is ruby. That is the only piece of
   brand trivia allowed to do emotional work.
5. **Sound-off first.** If a line needs to be heard rather than read, it fails the
   6" bumper and probably fails everywhere.

## Legal

- Every 0.0 claim goes through the Portman Group code. No exceptions.
- "119.5 seconds" is a factual claim and needs substantiation before air. If it
  does not land, the super becomes **THE SAME WAIT**.
- No line may imply that drinking 0.0 enables driving, or that it is a health product.

## Test

Read the line out loud in an empty room. If it sounds like an advert, rewrite it.
If it sounds like something the barman would say while pouring, keep it.
