What you can do
on Flowstate.

Plan a tripMaps

Pin places, build the budget, and draft the itinerary side by side — ask “what if we add a night?” and the total redoes itself.

Research a topicBoard

Pull articles, videos, and notes onto one board, then ask questions across all of them at once.

Build a mini toolLive

Turn a question into a working calculator or widget you can keep on the canvas and reuse.

Run a job huntTrack

Track roles, tailor answers, and run the salary math — all in one place instead of ten tabs.

Study somethingLearn

Collect explainers, turn them into notes and flashcards, then quiz yourself right where you learned it.
