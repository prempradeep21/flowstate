# Mobile App Pitch — outline

## Slide sequence

1. **Problem** — Rural families lack simple budgeting tools
2. **Solution** — Gullak: piggy-bank metaphor, local languages
3. **Market** — 300M+ underserved households in India
4. **Product** — Expense tracking, goals, insights (offline-first)
5. **Traction** — Beta in 2 districts, 4.6★ internal NPS
6. **Roadmap** — v1.0 Android → v1.5 iOS → v2.0 shared accounts
7. **Team** — PM, 2 mobile, 1 backend, design
8. **Ask** — 6-month runway to public launch
